Venue

International conference center
Patio 44, 4 Pl. Jussieu 55
75005, Paris, France

The Conference Center is close to Jussieu metro station (lines 7 and 10).

Access

From the main entrance of Jussieu, turn right and then left towards Tower 44. Take the stairs up to the first floor. On the first floor, follow the corridor 44-45, and look for Room 108, where the conference will be held. Please follow the signs indicating the path to the venue.
