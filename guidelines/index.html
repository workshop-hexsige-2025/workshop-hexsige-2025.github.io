Talk Guidelines

Please meet the organizers during the break before your session to check your presentation. We kindly ask you to bring your presentation file on a USB key or send it to us by email before the beginning of the workshop. If you prefer to use your own laptop, please come in advance to verify compatibility with the projection system.

The duration of each type of talk is as follows:

  • Keynote talk: 45 minutes + 5 minutes for questions (50 minutes total).
  • Invited talks: 35 minutes + 5 minutes for questions (40 minutes total).
  • Contributed talks: 15 minutes + 5 minutes for questions (20 minutes total).

Poster Guidelines

The poster session is scheduled for Monday after the lunch break. Please take advantage of the Monday morning coffee break to set up your poster. Posters will remain displayed until the end of the workshop.

Chairperson Guidelines

We kindly ask session chairs to meet the organizers just before their session to receive additional information and instructions.
