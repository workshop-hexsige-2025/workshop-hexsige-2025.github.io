Abstract submission

Abstract submission is possible at this link.
Please, for each abstract, prepare and upload a document file (maximum 1 page) in an editable text format such as .doc, .docx, .odt or other commonly used editable formats.

Your document should include:

  • Title starting with O- for an oral presentation or P- for a poster presentation, according to your preference. Example: O-Title of the Abstract
  • The full names and complete institutional affiliations of all authors.
  • All figures included should be accompanied with a caption.
